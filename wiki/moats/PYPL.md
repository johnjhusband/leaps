# PayPal (PYPL) — Moat: NO

**Verdict: no moat.** Cheap on earnings, but a value trap.

- **Pricing power:** weak. Payments is a commoditizing, low-switching-cost business.
- **Competition:** Apple Pay, Stripe, Adyen, Block, bank-owned rails (Zelle), and stablecoins can all
  replicate the core service. "Competition can pop up immediately and suck away the business."
- **Brandon's call:** *"PayPal has no moat... it's a good price, but it's not a good company... PayPal's
  going to be a no."*
- **Why it screens cheap anyway:** low forward P/E (~7) — the golden line reads that as undervalued, but
  the low multiple reflects the eroding moat, not a bargain. This is exactly the value-trap the moat gate exists to catch.

**Action:** removed from the buy list by the moat gate.
