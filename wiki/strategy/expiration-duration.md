# Expiration / Duration

**Resolved rule (CORRECTED 2026-06-07):** long duration — **~1 year on average** (*"I sell longer duration puts roughly call it one year on average"*), ranging **1 to ~2.5 years** (longer when cheaper), 6-month floor. Earlier I said a flat "~2 years" — overstated. Same for bought calls (6-month min, usually a LEAP). Accept low theta in exchange for high probability and EPS-growth runway — one 2-year put (~$30k) beats winning ~12 monthly puts in a row. Long duration enables rolling down-and-out and waiting out any drawdown.
