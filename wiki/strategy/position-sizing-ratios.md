# Position Sizing & Ratios

**Resolved rule (risk core):** Use portfolio-secured puts — base portfolio is collateral, no parked cash. Track 'sold-put assignment value' = total cash owed if every sold put were assigned at once.

**CORRECTED 2026-06-07 — the actual metric:** assignment value ÷ **"7-day liquidity"** (cash you can raise in 7 days across accounts — *"come up with $2 million in seven days"*), NOT total portfolio value. **Max ratio is regime-dependent:** ~30–40% now, **0–50% in a lofty market, up to 75% (≈100% COVID-cheap)**; he **raises** the cap as the market falls. Survives a **50–70%** crash (obligation fixed, liquidity halves → ratio doubles, still coverable by selling the base). Assigned **~4× in ~10 years**. Per-name **call** exposure capped ~**3–5%** (UNH 1%, NVDA 3%, Meta 4%); never 20–40% of the account in one name's calls.
