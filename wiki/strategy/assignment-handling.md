# Assignment Handling

**Resolved rule:** Assignment is rare (~3–4x in 12 years). If assigned: (a) sell base-portfolio shares to fund it, (b) take assignment on margin, or (c) roll down-and-out to a cheaper strike + further expiration. You can roll down-and-out nearly forever on an index/quality company because EPS growth returns and the contract expires worthless; you cannot roll up-and-out forever.

See canonical [[strategy-spec]]. Full deduped evidence: `_meta/digests/assignment-handling.md`.
